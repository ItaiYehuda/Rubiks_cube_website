CFOP Method

CFOP stands for:

  • Cross: Create a cross shape on one face (usually white).
  • F2L (First Two Layers): Solve the first two layers simultaneously by inserting corner-edge pairs.
  • OLL (Orientation of Last Layer): Make the opposite face (usually yellow) all one color.
  • PLL (Permutation of Last Layer): Rearrange the last layer to complete the cube.
